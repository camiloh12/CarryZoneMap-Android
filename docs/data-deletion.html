Account & Data Deletion Request

App: CCW Map

Last Updated: November 4, 2025

Request Account Deletion

We respect your right to have your account and associated data deleted. This page explains what data will be deleted and how to submit a deletion request.

Submit Deletion Request

To delete your account and all associated data, please send an email to:

Request Account Deletion

or email: camilo@kyberneticlabs.com

What Data Will Be Deleted

When you request account deletion, the following data will be permanently removed:

Account Information

  • Email address - Your registered email
  • User ID - Your unique account identifier
  • Authentication data - Hashed password and session tokens

User-Generated Content

  • All pins you created - Location coordinates and status markers
  • Pin metadata - Timestamps and associations with your account

Local Data

  • Offline data - Any locally cached pins and account information on your device
  • App preferences - Settings and preferences (automatically removed when app is uninstalled)

Deletion Process & Timeline

  1. Submit Request

    Send an email to camilo@kyberneticlabs.com with the subject "Account Deletion Request" and include the email address associated with your account.

  2. Verification

    We may send a confirmation email to verify your identity and ensure the request is legitimate.

  3. Processing Time

    Your data will be deleted within 30 days of receiving your verified request.

  4. Confirmation

    You will receive an email confirmation once your account and data have been permanently deleted.

IMPORTANT: Account deletion is permanent and irreversible. Once deleted:

  • You cannot recover your account or data
  • All pins you created will be removed from the map
  • You will need to create a new account if you wish to use the app again

Alternative: Delete Specific Pins

If you only want to delete specific pins (not your entire account), you can do so directly in the app:

  1. Open the CCW Map app
  2. Tap on the pin you want to delete
  3. Select "Delete" from the pin dialog

Data Not Covered by Deletion

The following data may be retained for legal or operational reasons:

  • Anonymous usage statistics - Non-identifiable analytics data (if applicable)
  • Legal records - Data required for compliance with legal obligations
  • Backup archives - May take up to 90 days to purge from all backup systems

Questions or Issues?

If you have questions about the deletion process or encounter any issues, please contact us:

  • Email: camilo@kyberneticlabs.com
  • GitHub: CarryZoneMap-Android
